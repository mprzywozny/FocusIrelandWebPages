Get involved

Want to do something exciting and help raise money for Focus Ireland? Check out our fundraising events from climbing Carrauntoohil, to sleeping out, or hosting a coffee morning. Find out how to get started here.

The Journey Home

Join Team Focus Ireland at Iarnród Éireann train stations this Christmas for The Journey Home.

Take The Plunge For Focus Ireland

Take The Plunge for Focus Ireland on Saturday 25th November at various beautiful shores around Ireland. Support Focus Ireland by dipping or swimming in the cold seas at Garryvoe Beach, Co. Cork, Tramore Beach, Co. Waterford and Old Head Beach, Louisburgh, Co. Mayo.

Christmas Jumper Day

Get into the Christmas spirit of fun & festivities while raising much needed funds for Focus Ireland. Join us and jingle your jumper!

Limerick Christmas Concert Of Hope 2023

Focus Ireland are delighted to announce that we will hold our Limerick Christmas Concert in Saint Mary’s Cathedral on Monday 11 December at 7.30pm.

Dublin Marathon

Take on the challenge of the Dublin Marathon as part of team Focus Ireland this October 29th.

Berlin Marathon

Join team Focus Ireland at the Berlin Marathon this September. Run 42.2km through the historic city and raise vital funds for people experiencing homelessness.

Camino de Santiago

Join Focus Ireland on the Camino de Santiago in 2023. This fully guided 103km trek from Vigo to Santiago is not one to be missed!

Warriors Run

Take on the challenge of the sold-out Warrior's Run for Focus Ireland. This 15k challenge takes place in Strandhill Co Sligo on August 26.

Skydiving

Join team Focus Ireland by skydiving with the Irish Parachute Club, based in Clonbullogue airfield, Co. Offaly.

VHI Women’s Mini Marathon

Take on the VHI Women's Mini Marathon this June Bank Holiday Sunday. Run or walk 10km and raise vital funds for Focus Ireland.

Machu Picchu

Travel to the lost city of Machu Picchu while supporting Focus Ireland.

Cork City Marathon

Join Focus Ireland for the Cork City marathon on Sunday June 5th. Run the full marathon, half marathon or get a group together and run it as a relay.

Kilimanjaro

Take on the challenge of Mount Kilimanjaro with Focus Ireland.

Birthday Fundraiser

Do you have a birthday coming up? Why not have a birthday fundraiser in aid of Focus Ireland. Ask your friends and family to donate instead of presents this year.
